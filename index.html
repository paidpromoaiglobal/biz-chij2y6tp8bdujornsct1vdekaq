About Us

ABHYAAS HINDI CLASS

Abhyaas Hindi Class is a trusted Hindi language coaching centre located in Bhuvaneshwari Nagar, Velachery, Chennai. Founded and led by the highly dedicated Vibha Ojha Ma'am, the institute specialises in helping students of all ages – especially school-going children – develop strong reading, writing, and spoken Hindi skills from the ground up. With a perfect 5-star rating and a warm, nurturing teaching environment, Abhyaas has become the go-to destination for Hindi learning in the neighbourhood.

What sets Abhyaas Hindi Class apart is the personalised attention every student receives. Classes are available 7 days a week, with flexible timings to suit busy family schedules. Whether your child is a complete beginner or needs to strengthen their Hindi for school exams, Vibha Ma'am's patient and engaging teaching methodology ensures rapid progress and growing confidence. Parents consistently praise her dedication, professionalism, and the remarkable results their children achieve within just a few months of joining.
